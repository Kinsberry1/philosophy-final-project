Virtue Ethics & My Career

Applying Aristotle's Ethics to My Journey as a QA/SDET

Project Overview

This philosophy project explores virtue ethics and how it influences ethical responsibility in a tech career. Aristotle emphasized character and habits, which parallels the consistent, ethical behavior needed in quality assurance.

Reflection

Virtue ethics encourages integrity and accountability—qualities that translate to transparent bug reporting, reliable testing, and long-term thinking. As an SDET, I believe ethics is not just academic—it’s how we ensure trust in the systems we build and test.
