About Me

I found a surprising strength in being the 'devil's advocate,' challenging ideas to gain new perspectives.

My long-term goal is to keep building my knowledge, master the skills I've started learning, and push myself beyond any limits. Much like Batman, my journey is not without struggle, but it is defined by resilience, internal strength, and the relentless pursuit of becoming better every day.
