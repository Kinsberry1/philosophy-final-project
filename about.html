About Me

My name is Keith Kingsberry. I am currently studying computer science with the goal of building a better future for myself. I originally pursued manual QA to break into IT and then earned an SDET certification, which I now understand is only the foundation of my true potential.

Through the Introduction to Philosophy course, I learned that my personal views and real-world experiences hold significant value. Philosophy helped me realize how critical thinking and questioning align naturally with who I am. I found strength in taking the role of the 'devil's advocate,' challenging ideas to gain a deeper understanding of diverse perspectives.

My long-term goal is to continue expanding my knowledge, strengthening the skills I've begun to build, and pushing myself beyond any limits. Much like Batman, my journey is defined by resilience, inner strength, and the relentless pursuit of becoming better every day, despite the struggles and setbacks.
